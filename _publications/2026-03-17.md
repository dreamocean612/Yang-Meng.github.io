This work proposes AffordRAG-Factory, a hierarchical embodied multi-robot task execution framework for smart manufacturing under limited edge-side computing resources. By combining small vision-language models with knowledge graph retrieval and constraint reasoning, the framework transforms ambiguous worker instructions into explainable, verifiable, and executable robot commands across heterogeneous robotic platforms.
